About Us

Diamond prices are driven by supply-demand dynamics, economic conditions, and trends in consumer preferences. While global demand, especially in markets like China and India, supports price stability, the rise of synthetic diamonds and changing market preferences for ethical sourcing could create downward pressure, particularly for lower-quality stones. In the short term, prices may experience moderate growth, but long-term forecasts remain uncertain due to these shifting factors and technological innovations in diamond production.
